Reducing Carbon Footprint in eCommerce Logistics with the Carbon Footprint Calculator

Industry: eCommerce
Product: Carbon Footprint Calculator
Objective: Enables eCommerce retailers to accurately calculate and reduce carbon emissions from logistics.
Overview

As environmental sustainability becomes increasingly vital for businesses, eCommerce retailers face challenges in reducing their carbon footprint. Logistics, particularly the transportation of goods, significantly contributes to carbon emissions. To help tackle this issue, We at Clavrit helped our customer develop a Carbon Footprint Calculator—a tool designed specifically to measure and mitigate the carbon emissions produced by logistics in the eCommerce industry.

Solution

Carbon Footprint Calculator isa robust solution aimed at providing retailers with detailed data on their carbon emissions from logistics. The tool collects data from various logistics touchpoints, including shipping routes, warehouse locations, and transport methods, then uses a proprietary algorithm to estimate the carbon footprint for every order shipped.

The calculator was seamlessly integrated with various Commerce platforms, enabling real-time analysis for retailers directly from their eCommerce platforms. The data allows retailers to track their environmental impact and make informed decisions about their shipping processes, helping them not only report but also actively reduce their emissions.

Results

Accurate Reporting: Retailers using the Carbon Footprint Calculator saw an immediate improvement in their ability to track logistics-related carbon emissions with precision.

Sustainability Optimization: By identifying the most carbon-intensive aspects of their logistics operations, retailers were able to make strategic adjustments.

Improved Customer Engagement: Retailers reported that the tool helped strengthen relationships with eco-conscious consumers. By being transparent about their efforts to reduce carbon emissions, these businesses could enhance brand loyalty and appeal to a growing market segment that prioritizes sustainability.

Revolutionizing Fashion Rentals for a Sustainable Future

Product/ Solution: Rental Fashion for Circular Economy
Industry: Fashion & Lifestyle
Objective: Create a sustainable marketplace for fashion rentals, offering brands a new revenue channel while promoting long-term sustainability.
Overview

The customer was interested to build a revolutionary platform that introduces a sustainable way for brands to offer rental services to customers. By developing a marketplace that caters to both consumers and brands, the platform allows brands to rent out their products, giving them control over inventory while generating additional revenue. Simultaneously, customers can enjoy a personalized cloud wardrobe, with the freedom to rent and return fashion items on a global scale.

Solution

Clavrit helped the customer develop a unique solution with its ” Keep Your Stock” model, allowing brands to retain control over their inventory while tapping into the growing rental market. Through a data-driven approach, the platform provides insights into customer rental behaviours, helping brands optimize their product portfolio for profitability and long-term sustainability. Additionally, it offers a global pick-up-and-drop service, making it convenient for travellers to access their favourite brands anywhere in the world.

Clavrit also developed a Resilience Engine, an AI-powered tool that analyses demand forecasting as a function of price and quality. This engine helps brands identify their “hero” products—those with the highest profitability in the rental market—and build a resilient, profitable product portfolio.

Results

New Revenue Streams: Brands successfully unlocked rental as a new revenue channel, increasing profitability without sacrificing product control.

Optimized Product Portfolio: By identifying the most resilient and in-demand products, brands were able to focus on quality over quantity, reducing waste and achieving long-term sustainability.

Sustainability Milestones: With a focus on reducing carbon footprints through local circulation points and optimizing supply chains, the platform helped both brands and customers contribute to a more sustainable fashion industry.

Such plants can be attached to hospitals do ensure seamless supply of medical oxygen to all wards, from neonatal to emergency wards. Results

Inventory Tracking: Real-time tracking of generated oxygen and optimized production based on demand. Visualize patient cases and diagnosis summary.

Preventive Maintenance: Accurate forecasts of equipment’s maintenance. To prevent failures and reduce operation costs. Visualizes equipment’s run-time, previous maintenance summary and possible scenarios causing failure.
