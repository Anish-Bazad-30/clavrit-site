Industry

Entry Management

Product

A Unified Ticketing and Entry Platform

Objective

Enable privacy-first fraud defense and real-time commercial optimization across venues while automating support and unifying operations end-to-end.

Problem
  • Rising multi-vector fraud (account takeovers, bot scalping, resale abuse) with fragmented venue data and strict privacy regulations hinder collaborative defenses.
  • Volatile demand driven by weather, social sentiment, and mobility leads to static pricing, low fill-rate, and guest dissatisfaction during spikes or dips.
  • Disconnected ticketing, POS, distribution, and support workflows inflate operational overhead and slow guest issue resolution.
Solution
  • Federated fraud learning across venues with differential privacy and secure aggregation to share model updates, not raw data; deploy anomaly models at entry and checkout to flag risky behaviors in real time.
  • Minute-by-minute ticket dynamicization blending weather feeds, social signals, and mobility data to auto-adjust prices, bundles, and access rules for conversion and experience.
  • AI helpdesk for omni-channel FAQs and operational status; unify ticketing, packaging, POS, dynamic pricing, distribution, and partner inventory under a single operations layer.
Results
  • 30–50% fewer false positives and improved fraud catch-rate by learning from broader cross-venue patterns without violating data privacy norms.
  • 5–12% lift in fill-rate and higher guest satisfaction during demand shocks via responsive pricing and access policies.
  • Faster resolution times and lower support load, alongside higher conversion from an integrated operations stack spanning checkout to entry.
