Case Study 1

Estimate your next Agile Project with Generative AI

Industry: Project Management
Product: Manage Agile Projects to Budget
Objective: Simplify agile team performance tracking with using the power of Generative AI
Overview

Software projects on average run 66 percent over budget and 33 percent over schedule, making it difficult for business executives to manage stakeholder expectations. Project managers rely heavily on project collaboration tools for managing tasks, but find it difficult to track team performance against an accurate project baseline.We created Baseliner.ai to measure and track team performance with an accurate baseline across your project portfolio.Baseliner.ai combines Generative-AI and 3-point estimation to establish an accurate project baseline to track team performance to task estimates.

Solution

Baseliner.ai tracks team performance with accurate project baselines powered by Clavrit’s Generative AI expertise. Users can sync tasks from Jira or upload them via Excel, including descriptions and labels for AI analysis.

Using a 3-Point estimation technique and Generative AI, Baseliner.ai calculates risk and uncertainty, giving early alerts when tasks need attention so teams can stay on track across their entire project portfolio.

Results

Radical transparency: Baseliner.ai allows stakeholders to visualize the impact of scope and time project changes in real-time against budget performance.

Tracking team performance: Baseliner.ai allows project managers to sync actual time spent and track task estimates with easy visual graph representation to measure team productivity.

Seamless integration: Baseliner.ai provides integration to Jira and Excel, ensuring tasks are kept in sync with your team’s day-to-day task management and planning activities.

Case Study 3

Optimizing processes through AI

Industry: Healthcare
Product: Solar energy-based Oxygen Generation
Objective: Create AI Dashboards for optimization, preventive maintenance and sustainability
Overview

The customer provides integrated, solar-powered oxygen plants that ensure a reliable and eco-friendly medical oxygen supply. They partner with hospitals and home care agencies to deliver consistent oxygen access, even in remote areas with limited healthcare infrastructure. These plants support all hospital wards, from neonatal to emergency, meeting the critical need for uninterrupted oxygen delivery.

Solution

Clavrit partners with the customer to drive digital transformation and streamline the entire process, from onboarding facilities and managing inventory to scheduling deliveries. By eliminating inefficiencies and optimizing the flow of medical oxygen, we help ensure that no patient is left waiting when it matters most.

We have implemented user-friendly software and digital platforms that enable healthcare facilities to easily register patients, track oxygen levels, schedule refills, and coordinate deliveries, ensuring a smooth and error-free supply chain. Clavrit’s optimization, preventive maintenance, and sustainability dashboards provide real-time insights on availability, logistics, pre-orders, and key sustainability metrics like solar energy generation and carbon offset, helping the customer demonstrate their positive impact and meet social and environmental commitments.

Results

Inventory Tracking: Real-time tracking of generated oxygen and optimized production based on demand. Visualize patient cases and diagnosis summary.

Preventive Maintenance: Accurate forecasts of equipment’s maintenance. To prevent failures and reduce operation costs. Visualizes equipment’s run-time, previous maintenance summary and possible scenarios causing failure.

Impact Analytics: This sustainability dashboard visualizes insights between solar and an electricity powered plant on relative cost savings, efficiency improvements and better customer experience.
