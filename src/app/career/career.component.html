Perks and Benefits!!!

Clavrit offers benefits in order to encourage a culture of joy & self-accomplishment by catering to individual needs with respect to flexibility, career enhancement and an enriched personal life. Our goal is to make Clavrit an employee-friendly company, and we strive hard to maintain transparency, diversity, and equal opportunities for everyone.

Flexible Work Hours

At Clavrit, we value productivity and believe it thrives with a balanced personal life. We offer flexibility in work hours to help employees meet family commitments. This ensures an enriched balance between work and life for everyone.

Work Culture

At Clavrit, we ensure everyone can embrace their unique identity and cultural experiences. We foster an environment where people can truly be themselves. This helps them thrive both professionally and personally.

Work Life Balance

Our employees enjoy a perfect work-life balance at Clavrit, we offer perks like birthday leaves, time off from work etc encouraging employees to relax & rejuvinate.

Equal Opportunities

Clavrit is an equal opportunity employer that welcomes talent from all backgrounds without discrimination. We value freedom of thought, transparent communication, and diversity. Our people enjoy ample opportunities to grow and advance their careers.

Stimulating Work Environment

At Clavrit, you’ll have opportunities to sharpen your skills across diverse specializations. Our variety of work and experiences provide a strong platform for career growth. We foster a welcoming environment where new ideas and exploration are encouraged.

Career Progression

At Clavrit, we aim for uncompromised perfection through creativity and constant improvement. We prioritize keeping our teams updated with the latest technology and trends. Regular training and seminars help our people learn and grow in their domains.

We Care

At Clavrit, our employees enjoy comprehensive health benefits and group insurance for peace of mind. We offer financial support when needed and regularly give back to local NGOs. We also empower employees’ families through vocational training and skill development.

Remote Work

At Clavrit, we offer diverse work options — onsite, hybrid, and remote. We stand out for providing Work From Anywhere (WFA) all year round, even post-COVID. This flexibility lets you choose how and where you work best.

Acclaim & Accolades

At Clavrit, we know appreciation boosts self-confidence and drives top performance. We make it a priority to recognize and reward employees who go above and beyond. From incentives to awards, we ensure our people feel valued for their contributions.

We are excited to discover the unique skills and perspectives that you can bring to Clavrit!
