Case Study 1

Reducing Carbon Footprint in eCommerce Logistics with the Carbon Footprint Calculator

Industry: eCommerce
Product: Carbon Footprint Calculator
Objective: Enables eCommerce retailers to accurately calculate and reduce carbon emissions from logistics
Problem
  • eCommerce retailers couldn’t clearly see how much carbon their deliveries were producing
  • There was no easy, automated way to track or measure emissions, especially for last-mile delivery
Solution
  • Developed a web-based Carbon Footprint Calculator for logistics emissions tracking
  • Integrated live data from shipping partners to enable real-time carbon scoring
  • Connected seamlessly with eCommerce platforms to help retailers monitor, report, and reduce emissions

Results

  • Improved accuracy to track logistics related carbon emissions
  • Identified and reduced most carbon intensive areas in logistics
  • Increased customer trust by being transparent about carbon reduction efforts

Case Study 2

Revolutionizing Fashion Rentals for a Sustainable Future

Product/ Solution: Rental Fashion for Circular Economy
Industry: Fashion & Lifestyle
Objective: Create a sustainable marketplace for fashion rentals, offering brands a new revenue channel while promoting long-term sustainability.
Problem
  • Lack of a unified marketplace connecting brands and consumers for fashion rentals
  • Limited access for customers to rent and return fashion items flexibly across locations
Solution
  • Developed a rental platform using the “Keep Your Stock” model, allowing brands to retain inventory control
  • Enabled data-driven insights into customer rental behavior for product portfolio optimization
  • Introduced a global pick-up and drop-off service for convenient cross-border rentals
  • Built the Resilience Engine, an AI tool for forecasting demand and identifying high-profit “hero” products

Results

  • Opened new revenue streams for brands through rental without losing inventory control
  • Helped brands build optimized product portfolios focused on high-demand, sustainable items
  • Supported sustainability goals by reducing waste and lowering carbon footprints through localized logistics and smarter supply chains

Case Study 3

Optimizing processes through AI and sustainability dashboards

Industry: Healthcare
Product: Solar energy-based Oxygen Generation
Objective: Help facility managers optimize operations, enable preventive maintenance, and improve sustainability through AI-powered dashboards.
Problem
  • Limited visibility into remote, solar-powered oxygen plant operations
  • Manual monitoring of critical, energy-intensive systems in remote areas
  • No unified dashboard to ensure high availability or generate predictive alerts for maintenance and supply continuity
Solution
  • Developed digital tools to onboard healthcare facilities, manage oxygen inventory, and schedule deliveries
  • Automated tracking, refills, and delivery workflows to streamline the oxygen supply chain
  • Built real-time dashboards for operational optimization, maintenance insights, and sustainability tracking

Results

  • Enabled real-time tracking of oxygen production and patient case visualization
  • Improved maintenance planning with predictive insights to avoid equipment failures and reduce costs
  • Delivered sustainability insights by comparing solar vs. electricity-powered plant performance for cost and efficiency benefits
